Scrambled Eggs

Description

Cooking isn't about always doing the right thing, and this summer scramble is a perfect example. Adding juicy,sweet cherry tomatoes to a scramble tastes greats, but it's not a common practice since having a pool of liquid under your eggs is considered bad form.

I don't care;I have toast.Try to find a got milk feta chees for this;it's awesome!

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • pepper flakes
  • 9 cherry tomatoes,halved
  • cheese
  • olive oil
  • 1 pinch sea salt

Steps

  1. Beat eggs and red pepper flackes together in a bowl.
  2. Heat olive oil in a nonstick pan.
  3. Cook and stir egg mixture until eggs are screambled and softly set.
  4. Transfer eggs to a plate and sprinkle with sea salt.
