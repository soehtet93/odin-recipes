Korean Ground Beef Bowl

Description

This Korean beef bowl is quick and easy to make.The ingredients can easily be adjusted to suit your taste. Serve over warm rice or spiralized vegetables.

I'd like to think these are perfectly fine to eat by themselves, but if you were to mix fresh vegetables or rice, it is also really delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 5 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tablespoon freshly granted ginger
  • 1/2 cup of soy sauce
  • 6 green onions
  • 4 cups hot cooked brown rice
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add beef and cook, stirring and crumbling into small pieces.
  3. Stir in garlic, ginger, and sesame oil and cook until fragrant, about 2 minutes.
  4. Add 1/2 of chopped green onions
  5. Serve over hot cooked rice; garnish with sesame seeds and remaining green onions.
